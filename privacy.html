This Privacy Policy describes how the YouTube Audio Only Chrome Extension ("the Extension") handles your data. 1. Information Collection The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or identifiable user information. We do not access: Your browsing history Your identity Any personal files or credentials The extension only uses Chrome APIs to modify YouTube's front-end behavior on pages you visit. 2. Local Storage of Preferences The Extension stores the following settings locally in your browser using chrome.storage.sync: Whether to hide YouTube video players (toggle) Whether to hide YouTube thumbnails (thumbblock) These settings are only used to maintain your preferences across sessions. 3. Permissions The Extension uses the following Chrome permissions: "activeTab" — to modify YouTube pages you are actively viewing "storage" — to save toggle settings locally No remote servers or external domains are contacted by the Extension. 4. Third-Party Services This Extension does not use any third-party services, trackers, or analytics tools. 5. Security All code runs locally in your browser. The extension does not transmit or share data with any external entity. 6. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here. 7.
